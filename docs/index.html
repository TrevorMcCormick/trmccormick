How to Study for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate Exam

2021-06-02  included in Certifications
Background There is no better preparation than hands-on experience with AWS. Before I attempted this certification exam, I had about two years of experience working across AWS, primarily using services to build my website and handle data ingestion and warehousing processes at work. I had drawn out some simple architecture documents up to this point, and I could generally describe what was happening. However, I knew that in order to pass the exam, I would need to strengthen my understanding of services I was already familiar with (S3, RDS, IAM), and spend a significant amount of time learning the ones I did not use day to day (VPC, Route53, Networking, etc).
